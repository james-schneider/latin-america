Welcome to El Salvador!

My Experience in El Salvador

Although you might hear on the news how dangerous El Salvador is, I have been there three times. I have never felt threatened or in danger. It's a big city with crime like most big cities, so you have take precautions.

There are many beautiful places to see: lakes, volcanoes, and ruins to name a few.

There are delicious foods to try as well. El Salvador's most notable dish is the pupusa. It is made with thick homemade corn flour or rice flour tortillas stuffed with the filling of your choice. You don't want to leave El Salvador without trying a fresh, hot pupusa.
